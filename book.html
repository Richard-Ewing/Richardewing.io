The Product Economist

Financial Fluency, AI Unit Economics, and Capital Allocation.

In every tech company, there is a "Senior Ceiling." High-performing leaders are illiterate in Capital Allocation. They view themselves as "builders," while their CEOs view them as "cost centers."

This book provides the "Rosetta Stone" of business: how to translate technical decisions into financial outcomes.

Core Concepts
  • Financial Conway's Law: Architecture inevitably mirrors the funding model.
  • The AI Tax: Modeling the hidden costs of tokens, compute, and APIs to prevent negative gross margins.
  • CapEx vs. OpEx: Auditing your roadmap to balance Growth Assets against Maintenance Tax.
