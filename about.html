The Origin Story

I did not start as an economist. I started in the trenches of product delivery. I watched millions of dollars burn in "Agile" furnaces—features built because we could, not because they were solvent.

When the GenAI wave hit, I realized the industry was walking into a trap. We were treating probabilistic, token-consuming agents like deterministic SaaS code. I saw companies scaling "success" metrics while hemorrhaging cash on every API call.

I created the Office of the Product Economist to formalize a discipline that didn't exist: The financial governance of non-deterministic software.

MY MANDATE

1. Define the Unit Economic Viability (UEV) of the asset.
2. Set the Kill Switch threshold.
3. Enforce the shutdown of insolvent assets.
