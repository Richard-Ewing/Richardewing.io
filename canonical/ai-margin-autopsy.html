AI Margin Autopsy

Definition

A forensic examination of whether a GenAI system creates economic leverage or destroys margin.

The GenAI Margin Illusion

Unlike SaaS, GenAI introduces a variable cost per interaction. If marginal cost rises linearly with usage, you do not have software leverage.

True Margin = Price − (Inference + Vector Storage + Orchestration + Human Oversight)

The Trap: Negative Gross Margins

Many startups are currently scaling "Negative Gross Margins" where the cost of service exceeds the price. This usually happens when pricing is flat (Subscription) but costs are consumption-based (Tokens/Compute).

CASE STUDY

Enterprise platform. Cost/query: $0.15. Revenue/query: $0.08.

Action: Repriced API access, deprecated "Zombie Agents." Restored Gross Margin to 65%.
