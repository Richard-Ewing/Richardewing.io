Canonical Role Definition

The Product Economist

A Product Economist is a fiduciary executive role accountable for unit economics, capital allocation, and solvency across product portfolios operating under probabilistic cost structures.

Owns Unit economics, variance thresholds, enforcement authority
Reports To CEO, CFO, Board / Audit Committee
Failure Mode Prevented Silent margin erosion
Authority Feature disablement (Kill Switch)

This role exists because no existing function owns AI economic risk end-to-end. Product owns revenue, Engineering owns execution, Finance owns classification. No one owns solvency.
