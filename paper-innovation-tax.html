The Innovation Tax

PAPER ID: WP-01 | DATE: JAN 2026 | CLASSIFICATION: DOCTRINE

Abstract: Legacy software is a shadow liability. Most organizations fail to account for the "carry cost" of code that generates no marginal value. We must deploy a "Governance of Subtraction".

1. The Carry Cost of Code

Every line of code in production is a liability, not an asset. It requires compute, human maintenance, and cognitive load. When features do not generate revenue in excess of their total carry cost, they are not "features"—they are taxes.

2. Subtraction as Strategy

The role of the Product Economist is not to approve new build; it is to calculate the solvency of the existing estate. If a feature's unit economics are negative, the strategic imperative is deletion, not optimization.
